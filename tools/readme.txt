This font "Ikkle-4" is created by Jack Oatley.
AKA Bitty/Brixdee on http://www.dafont.com/profile.php?user=764521

This font is an evolution of my other font "Ikkle", which is a 3px limited font. Ikkle-4 however is 4px limited, this allows it to have more characters and differences between upper and lower cases. Primarily designed for pixel-art games that want to make use of very limited space.

It is 100% free to use, though credit is appreciated and it'd be cool to know where you used it. You must not claim it as your own.
